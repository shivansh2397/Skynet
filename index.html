About us

  • Established in 1999, Skynet Travels & Tours Pvt. Ltd is a multi-vertical organization. For over 25 years, Skynet Travels and Tours has been providing Quality Service to its clients and customers associated with us. Headquartered at Globe Centre, Jamshedpur, Jharkhand. We have a branch in Jamshedpur and a corporate office in New Delhi.
  • Skynet Travels and Tours is a renowned travel company specializing in facilitating spiritual journeys to the holy cities of Makkah and Madinah. With a rich legacy spanning over two decades, the company has established itself as a trusted name in the industry, catering to the diverse needs of pilgrims from around the globe.
  • Skynet Travels and Tours is dedicated to Excellence and ensuring Customer Satisfaction. We prioritize the comfort, convenience, and spiritual fulfillment of each pilgrim. Our comprehensive services are designed to provide a seamless and enriching experience, ensuring that every aspect of the pilgrimage is meticulously organized and taken care of.
  • At Skynet Travels and Tours, we adhere to the highest standards of Professionalism, Ethics, and Integrity. Our dedicated team of professionals is committed to deliver personalized and attentive services, ensuring that every aspect of the pilgrimage is meticulously planned and executed.
  • With a focus on fostering a spiritual and fulfilling journey, Skynet Travels and Tours continues to be a preferred choice for pilgrims seeking a Transformative and Enriching Experience in the holy cities.
